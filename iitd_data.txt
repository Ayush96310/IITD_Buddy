IIT DELHI ACADEMIC & HOSTEL RULES (2025 EDITION)

1. HOSTEL TIMINGS
- The curfew for all hostels (Aravali, Kumaon, Himadri, etc.) is 12:30 AM.
- Late entry between 12:30 AM and 1:30 AM is permitted with a fine of Rs. 500.
- Entry after 1:30 AM requires prior written permission from the Warden.

2. LIBRARY FINES (CENTRAL LIBRARY)
- General Books: Rs. 2 per day for the first week, Rs. 5 per day thereafter.
- Reference Books: Rs. 50 per hour. (Strictly enforced).
- Lost Book Policy: Student must replace the book or pay 3x the current market price.

3. SAC (STUDENT ACTIVITY CENTER)
- The Gym is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM to 9:00 PM.
- The Swimming Pool is closed on Mondays for cleaning.
- Booking the Seminar Hall requires approval from the Dean of Student Affairs (DoSA) 48 hours in advance.

4. ACADEMIC GRADING
- 'A' Grade: 10 points (Outstanding).
- 'E' Grade: 2 points (Poor - Re-examination required).
- 'F' Grade: 0 points (Fail).
- Minimum attendance required to sit for exams is 75%. Medical certificates can relax this to 65%.

5. INTERNET & LAN USAGE
- Monthly data limit per student via Proxy is 100 GB.
- Use of torrents/P2P on the academic network (Academic Area) leads to a 2-week ban.
